Chapter 1707 Soaring Snake



Kurt had always remained calm. His actions were unhurried but were carried out with care and confidence.

He was never that harsh when facing a weaker opponent.

However, the same couldn't be said when he met stronger foes that were close to his level. Everyone witnessed how confident and calculated his moves were when he faced Lawson.

Despite his precision in fighting, however, the audience still found his performance lackluster.

An image flashed before Kurt's eyes before he finally took action.

He was someone who cultivated the Future Constellation Kalpa. This cultivation method gifted him with the Blessed Buddha Land's skill that enabled him to see into the future: the Constellation Eyes.

As he poured life vitality into his eyes, he would be able to see into a specific moment in the future that was reflected in his pupils.

There were only two warriors who mastered this theurgy. One was Kurt himself, and the other was the Buddhist abbot of the Hidden Spirit Temple.

Kurt had the ability to see nine seconds into the future.

Half a minute more would be too blurry, while a full minute would simply yield him nothing.

The scene he saw half a minute later painted a horrible picture!

Tianming activated his extremely sharp theurgy and killed him!

The blur prevented Kurt from making out the exact theurgy Tianming used so he wouldn't be able to shield himself against it.

He could only take the initiative and launch the attack.

"Scorching Sun Theurgy!"

Kurt yelled. He stretched out his palms from which he launched a giant Buddhist wheel.

The Buddhist wheel shone brightly, like a blinding, dazzling sun.

There was actually no such thing as a Godly Genius in the cultivation of Buddhism.

Buddhist cultivation was divided into four levels of meditation.

It was only after the final, fourth level would one be finally able to practice the theurgy.

Only when a warrior was able to use a theurgy could he really live and breathe Buddhism. In a sense, he was a Godly Genius of the Godly Way.

Since Kurt had already gone beyond the fourth level and mastered the theurgy, he could then be considered as a Godly Genius of the Blessed Buddha Land.

The theurgy came in the form of thousands of golden palm prints that crashed down over Tianming.

Buddhism was grand.

It was not an exaggeration to say that this theurgy had the power to possibly ruin the world.

Even Luo Zheng, Nathan, and the other Godly Geniuses were shocked at this display of sheer power.

"This little monk... How could he be so powerful?"

"Scorching Sun Theurgy! He cultivated the most powerful theurgy of the Hidden Spirit Temple!"

"Warriors from the Blessed Buddha Land have always been uninterested in fighting but they have a profound heritage."

"That Tianming will be defeated by the grand Buddhist heritage."

The scene in front of them truly widened their horizons.

It was a mere battle between two Soul Sea Realm warriors but this sudden climax was extremely overwhelming.

A few Spirit Supreme Realm and Spirit Transformation Realm warriors even shivered. Not many of them were confident that they'd be able to withstand such a strong attack.

The speed at which the golden palms crashed down wasn't fast but they were as dense as the entire heavens crashing down on one's body.

"This theurgy is really strong," Tianming muttered worriedly. As the golden sky crashed down, he raised his long sword above his head.

A silver light emanated from the sword but it grew dimmer by the second.

This was the first time that the others looked at the sword properly.

It was normal, around 1.1 meters long.

The blade was two fingers wide with a wooden hilt and a ridge in the middle.

There was nothing special about this sword. It looked extremely ordinary.

However, beyond its simplistic design were two words carved onto the blade: 'Soaring Snake.'

The Soaring Snake.

This sword belonged to the Lord of Weapons.

It was Chiyou's* sword. (Chiyou is Chinese Weaponry God. According to legend he invented many deadly weapons.)

All the most powerful weapons, whether it might be a knife, spear, sword or halberd, were made by the Lord of Weapons.

The most powerful knife ever made was named the Tiger Soul.

Then there was also the sword named Soaring Snake.

There was a spear called the Fierce Killing.

Then a halberd by the name of the Devil Killing.

A set of armor named as the Luminous Light.

A crossbow called the Sky Piercing.

And lastly, a dagger-axe known as the Destructive War.

The Soaring Snake was considered as the strongest weapon among the supreme primordial weapons. It was also formerly a missing heritage of the Lord of Weapons, Chiyou.

"The Soaring Snake evolved into a dragon and the polestar twinkled. A noble man should act like a dragon and walk the way to justice!" Tianming chanted.

The Heavenly Sword he comprehended back then contained the Justice Godly Way.

Cultivating the Heavenly Sword Illustration was the only way to find Chiyou's treasure.

The Heavenly Sword Illustration that was discovered and spread all over the world was the key to such a powerful weapon. However, Tianming was the only one who succeeded in cultivating it after so many years.

He was called by the treasure into the savage land where he found it.

Tianming's Lotus Flower was the same as Zheng's at that time, blooming continually to the last petal.

He already knew his destiny.

The universe was just an interlude.

And this Illusion Battlefield was his ladder.

Buzz!

A streak of light flashed underneath Tianming and shot up high in the sky like a pillar.

This light pierced through the clouds, the star group, and even the Illusion Battlefield!

The pillar was seen by so many warriors in the universe as it rose up straight out of nowhere.

"Alas... Darn it." The mysterious girl stared at the light pillar that suddenly showed up beside her. "He actually found Chiyou's sword.

What change will happen in this round of grand era? It's really hard to figure out."

The thought depressed her, suddenly finding her job difficult to handle.

"What... an odd scene!"

"I've seen thirty-three apocalyptic astronomical phenomena and endless auspicious clouds, but this is the first time I ever saw a light pillar like this," a Supreme Lord remarked.

"What did he do?!"

"What a beautiful light pillar!"

Karl stood on top of the stone and was also shocked at the appearance of Tianming's golden pillar.

"A noble man... A noble man should act like a dragon!" he murmured.

Karl had been cultivating the Justice Godly Way!

His Lotus Flower had completely bloomed, however, his comprehension of 'justice' had gotten more and more radical. He also got lost for a time.

Seeing the Justice Godly Way Tianming activated was like seeing a light break through the darkness. He did not realize how off-path he had been until he saw the light.

Whoosh!

Tianming stepped forward and bathed himself in the light.

He held the Soaring Snake in front of his chest and faced those golden palm prints.

As he held his sword up, the sword suddenly emitted a dazzling sword light. This was the ultimate justice of the swordsmanship!

Splash!

The light emanated from the blade in waves.

Reflected in these waves were the golden palm prints.

As the two made contact, the waves shattered, like bright, dazzling glass.

It wasn't just the waves though. The palm prints also shattered at the same time!

The waves were weaved from Tianming's sword light.

Nobody had seen it coming.

Nobody realized that this Soaring Snake was actually everywhere.

Kurt's confidence broke and his brows furrowed at the events that had just transpired.

His shoulders slumped the moment he decided to yield to his opponent.

Because he already saw what would happen nine seconds later.

What would happen half a minute later was blurry because it was still uncertain, so Kurt would fight tooth and nail to change the fate.

However, what would happen nine seconds later was so clear, indicating its certainty. He could not do anything to change that.

The scene flashed before his eyes: it was him, covered by the waves similar to the ones produced by Tianming.

That was the moment Kurt knew that he was to die by Tianming's sword.

There was no need to resist.

Three seconds passed.

And then six.

Pretty soon, the nine seconds were up.

Everything was just like what his Constellation Eyes showed him.

The invisible sword intent skimmed through his body.

Tianming overpowered Kurt and eventually broke his Scorching Sun Theurgy.

The waves of light shattered and disappeared, along with Kurt's body.

The results were evidently clear: Tianming won!

The Heaven Earth Board quietly reconstructed itself, mending each line and point with white and black stones.

Tianming sheathed his Soaring Snake back into its scabbard.

There was a slight clink as his blade was put away while the entire universe was plunged into silence.

The performances of these Godly Geniuses were truly amazing.

There were ten of them and each had the potential to become the universe's top warriors in the world of chaos.

Kurt, Lawson, Karl, all of them.

This was a generation of amazingly powerful talents.

But what Tianming displayed just now was way beyond everyone's expectations.

"The new great world! This is truly the beginning of the new great world of chaos! Ha-ha!" someone shouted in glee and broke the silence.

The warriors, who had initially been stunned by the battle's outcome, finally came to their senses.

"Is he... really from... your Cloud Hall?" Preston asked, not really believing what he witnessed just now.

He was a Spirit Transformation Realm master, two realms higher than Tianming. But he knew that he would even lose to this young man if he fought with him.

How in the world could he do that though?

He was two realms below!

"Right!"

Ning Yudie nodded with a smile. The pride was evident on her face.
