Chapter 468 Number One Peak



There were many reasons why Luo Zheng admired Tianming. One of them was his humility.

Since Tianming was born from nothing, it wasn't always easy for him to stay grounded after he achieved great success in practicing.

Some disciples who were also born from nothing became extremely proud after having made some achievements. They regarded themselves as the pride of heaven. They thought that they were so virtuous and talented as if nobody on earth could beat them. Thus, they gradually slacked off in practicing. Even if they were talented disciples, their arrogance slowly diminished their will of martial arts--and they became just mediocre disciples with time.

However, Tianming wasn't conceited or rash. He still had a good attitude and state of mind even though Zheng's performance had made him feel hopeless and desperate. He didn't feel discouraged at all. He even told Luo Zheng that he would try his best to follow in his footsteps even though he couldn't surpass him.

Maybe other people were more gifted than Tianming, but only a few of them were as benevolent as him.

Now, although Tianming had spoken modestly, his move with the sword wasn't modest at all. Luo Zheng wouldn't dare underestimate this move.

The Heavenly Sword of Tianming could originally use the extremely terrifying power of heavens. After he had comprehended the consummate sword intent, his sword intent became even fuller and fiercer.

More importantly, he had comprehended the power of thunder in the Sword Washing Pool!

Now, he had both the powers of thunder and the heavens!

When the two of them were combined, the power of the sword would be equal to the punishment of heaven!

Meanwhile, Zheng's face stiffened in horror as he looked at the sword with the punishment from heaven.

The sword spirit, Lily, instantly spilt into small fragments of sword intent under Zheng's control and attached herself to the Streamer Sword in Zheng's hand.

In reality, Luo Zheng wasn't that skilled in controlling the sword spirit. If he could use the sword spirit well, he didn't need to attach Lily to the Streamer Sword after he called her out. He could attach her directly to the Streamer sword through his body.

However, it was just a skill. Luo Zheng would control it in time as he became better in using the sword spirit.

Lily's malicious Mu Ning was quite strong, and it was unknown how many creatures she had killed to have such a powerful malicious Mu Ning.

When she stood next to Luo Zheng, she was able to contain all of the malicious Mu Ning in her body. However, the strong and fierce malicious Mu Ning suddenly burst out when she was attached to the Streamer Sword.

A butcher who slaughtered cows and pigs everyday would have an increasing amount of malicious Mu Ning in his body as time passed. This kind of Mu Ning was relatively weak, and only sensitive people could feel it.

Others wouldn't say he looked murderous, but most people couldn't feel this kind of weak malicious Mu Ning anyway.

Dogs were always the most sensitive to the malicious Mu Ning. It was why they always barked at butchers when they saw them on the street. The malicious Mu Ning that emanated from the butchers made the sensitive dogs quite afraid.

Thus, it followed that malicious Mu Ning posed a threat to creatures. It had a stronger negative vibes compared to other kinds of strengths and auras.

For example, just a look from the war veterans who had fought for a long time, would make some people feel absolutely terrified. That was the kind of effect that the malicious Mu Ning had.

At present, the malicious Mu Ning in Zheng's body wasn't that strong. Although he had entered the Killing Sword Mountain due to fate and passively chose the killing way to cultivate his sword skills, the malicious Mu Ning couldn't be gathered in just one night.

He had just started practicing the killing sword skill and his malicious Mu Ning was still weak, but it didn't affect his usage of Lily's malicious Mu Ning.

As soon as Lily was attached to the Streamer Sword, the turbulent malicious Mu Ning instantly burst out.

The crowd felt that it was a demon, and not a human who stood in front of them, under the influence of the malicious Mu Ning.

And, the devil was so cruel that he would cut off their heads at any time and in any place.

Some timid disciples even felt their legs tremble at the presence of the strong malicious Mu Ning. Their knees buckled and they crumpled down to the ground.

"Oh my!

Luo Zheng became a devil!"

Some disciples began to shout hysterically under the malicious Aura's influence.

"What a horrible killing Mu Ning! What exactly has he gone through? He didn't have the malicious Mu Ning before, but why did he suddenly have a strong malicious Mu Ning now?" some of them exclaimed.

However, they were still clearly awed by the sight.

After a while, the two powerful auras instantly collided with each other.

Tianming's sword symbolized the power of heaven. He looked like he was an angel from heaven at that moment, sent to punish the criminals of the world for their sins.

On the other hand, Luo Zheng was like a devil raised against the heavens. His killing sword skill seemed like it could slaughter every creature in the world! To be the god of heaven through killing! To resist the strength of heaven through killing!

"Bang!"

A loud, booming noise came from the two powerful swords when they collided with each other.

The thunder and lightning, which looked like a punishment from heaven, hit Luo Zheng with sword intent after sword intent. In turn, the malicious Mu Ning in Zheng's Streamer Sword turned into spools of long threads and charged at the thunder and lightning as well. Some of the long threads shattered into pieces when they crashed against the thunder and lightning.

However, the long threads seemed to flow endlessly. After the thunder and lightning broke a series of the threads, another wave of threads burst out and rushed towards Tianming.

After a while, the abrasion from the countless long threads had weakened the power of Tianming's sword. The malicious Mu Ning finally disintegrated the surging thunder and lightning not long after.

The killing sword skill was a highly strong and powerful skill of swordsmanship. Luo Zheng didn't get to practice the cultivation method of the malicious Mu Ning, so he couldn't fully use the power of the killing sword skill--but he still defeated the sword of Tianming. Had he practiced the cultivation method that matched the malicious Mu Ning, its power would've been ten times stronger than it had been just now.

Although the killing sword skill was strong and powerful, it had a disadvantage. If the practitioner's body had too much malicious Mu Ning in it, demons would consume him and in the end, he might be destroyed by the malicious Mu Ning. Fortunately, Luo Zheng wouldn't have such trouble since he had a resonance between his soul consciousness and the Killing Sword Mountain.

After his move with the sword was defeated by Luo Zheng, Tianming took back the Blood-corroding Sword and returned it to its sheath.

However, the winner was still in doubt when Tianming took back his sword.

Then again, Tianming still chose to take the sword back and end the competition. It wasn't because he didn't want to fight anymore, but rather, he knew that Luo Zheng would also try his best if he gave his all in the fight--but he would still lose in the end.

He would do his best to fight back if his opponent was roughly as strong as him, because he might have a chance to win. However, Tianming had already lost to Luo Zheng when they comprehended the consummate sword intent. Moreover, his strength was far behind Zheng's now.

Thus, it was useless to fight with Luo Zheng anymore since he would definitely lose to him in the end anyway.

"I lost!" Tianming declared.

The referee who stood next to them heard Tianming and proclaimed the winner.

"Luo Zheng, the disciple of the Drizzle Peak won! And Tianming, the disciple of the Skytop Peak lost!" he announced.

"Number One! We are Number One!" someone shouted.

Several masters of Drizzle Peak happily clapped their hands and celebrated Zheng's victory with each other.

Meanwhile, Master Wen's eyes slightly moistened. Luo Zheng defeated Tianming in this competition and finally became the first out of all the disciples in thirty-three peaks.

Since Luo Zheng was ranked first among all the disciples in all the peaks, then Drizzle Peak became the first out of all the thirty-three peaks because of him!

The Drizzle Peak, which used to rank the last among all the peaks, suddenly became first! It was a miracle and it was all because of Luo Zheng!

The masters and all the disciples of Drizzle Peak couldn't believe the news. They thought it was unreal, as if they were in a sweet dream. If so, they hoped that they would never wake up from the dream.

The next and last opponent Luo Zheng had to fight was Patrick.

So far, no one had pushed Patrick to do his best in the competitions. He was the top disciple in Cloud Sect, only next to the extraordinarily talented Evan. It wasn't easy to gauge his real strength.

Luo Zheng clearly knew that it would be tough to win against Patrick in their upcoming competition.

Before he had comprehended the consummate sword intent, he wasn't that positive about his fight against Patrick. But now he had comprehended it, and the sword spirit Lily boosted his confidence in the competition.

However, it was still hard for Luo Zheng to determine what his chances were.

After all, he didn't know Patrick's real strength, and he didn't have a reference to measure it.

He just hoped that there would be someone powerful to fight against Patrick first.

Unfortunately, only a few disciples had the ability to challenge Patrick.

Wing was a man of great power, but Patrick defeated him in a second.

Jacques was powerful too, but Luo Zheng had already swallowed up his flood dragon bone spear so he couldn't do his best to fight Patrick anymore. As for Tracy, Luo Zheng thought that she wouldn't go all out to fight Patrick and surrender instead--although she tried her best and activated the body of the Blue Phoenix when she fought against him.

However, to Zheng's surprise, Tracy was picked in the draw lots to compete against Patrick on the battlefield.

Just as Luo Zheng had guessed, Tracy cheerfully surrendered as soon as they got on the battlefield.

'The girl is only cruel when she fights against me!' Luo Zheng thought and shook his head.

Fortunately, the Lord expressed his wish to look after Luo Zheng. Thus, the referee drew lots again after Tracy surrendered, and Patrick's name was drawn once more.

This time, he was slated to compete against Tianming!

A smile formed on Zheng's lips. Not everyone could force Patrick to do his best in the competition, but Tianming was the best man for the job.

However, Luo Zheng wondered how long Tianming would last, or if he could directly defeat Patrick.
