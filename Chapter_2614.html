Chapter 2614 Looking For Her Husband



Upon hearing the woman's response, those strong men burst into fits of uncontrollable laughter.

It didn't take them much effort to deceive this woman into coming with them. They thought she was a weirdo who wasn't cautious at all.

"Come with me then," the mid-rank True God said, leading the way.

The other proving godly warriors and the low-rank True God followed suit. The woman walked forward, surrounded by all those warriors.

It seemed as if she was being held hostage, but the woman didn't see anything wrong with what was going on.

The Cyan Demon Region territory was sparsely populated. After the city was abandoned, its ruins became a home for extremely tall and huge plants from the Swirl Forest.

Normally, weeds could only grow up to the knee, but over there, they towered the entire place and almost reached the skies and the sun.

On several occasions, they would hear strange bestial noises from the depths of the forest, which scared them.

Finally, the woman came to her senses. She murmured, "Er...where are you taking me?"

"To meet our leader!" the mid-rank True God answered with a mocking laugh.

His words made her rather unhappy. "I'm not here to meet your leader.

I'm going to the Swirl Forest."

"I know. Our leader knows where you can find it," the mid-rank True God explained.

As much as possible, the mid-rank True God didn't want any trouble. He tried his best not to get himself into conflict with that woman. The less trouble, the better. If she just followed him, then it would be much easier for all of them.

If she did something stupid, then he would be forced to take action.

"Okay," the woman responded. However, she had this look on her face.

'Something's not right, ' she thought.

Unfortunately, she was a little naive.

Back when she was still in the universe, she had a master who protected her even if she could crush countless warriors in the Illusion Battlefield using her own strength.

However, in the divine land, she didn't have as much control as before.

Even before she managed to sneak into the divine land, her master and fellow disciples had already given her tons of suggestions and words of advice. Those obviously didn't help because she was still confused and clueless.

Not long after, a broken wall with a height of over a thousand feet appeared before them. It was a large city before, and now it had become a nest of this group of people.

As the woman continued to follow them across the city wall, the mid-rank True God shouted, "Leader, I've found you a beautiful woman.

Would you believe me if I told you that she's a low-rank True God? I don't even know where she's from!" Then he laughed as if he just said the funniest thing.

As he kept on laughing, a few people began to appear from behind the broken bricks.

All of them were men. Most of them were proving godly warriors, while a few others were low-rank True Gods.

"A woman in the Cyan Demon Region? Unbelievable!"

"Her origin is unknown!"

"I haven't seen a woman for hundreds of years. Damn it!"

All of them gave her lustful stares.

Despite so, they all knew that she belonged to their leader. They could only look at her.

The woman winced when she realized what was actually going on. She was tricked by a bunch of bad guys.

As the woman grew frightened, the Godly Tile in her inner world began to flash intensely.

She was naive, but that didn't mean she was a fool. If she was, there was no way she could prove the Godly Way in such a short time like she had done.

Swish!

The purple light that emerged from her body immediately turned into four purple swords that circled in front of her.

The four swords rolled around, and within a radius of about a thousand feet, sand and stones flew in all directions. The proving godly warriors who had been standing behind her saw the situation go wrong, and they all scrambled to keep a certain distance from her.

The Gods Killing Sword Array formed by those four purple swords released a dangerous aura that alerted everyone.

The proving godly warriors couldn't help but panic.

Even the mid-rank True God had a hint of fear in his eyes.

He didn't expect such a foolish woman to possess that kind of strength.

It wasn't her strength that scared him. No matter how strapping she was, she was not a match for their leader.

It was impossible for any ordinary clan to train such a powerful low-rank True God. He was starting to think that she had some strong background.

The people in that place wouldn't survive anywhere else. If they offended any great force, it wouldn't take much time for their entire population to be wiped out.

Boom!

From a distance, a large and heavy stone board that was once on the ground instantly sprung up. Then, it flew through the air and smashed against the woman's head.

The woman nibbled lightly at her crimson-colored lips.

The sword before her vibrated.

The crowd didn't know how she did it. The enormous stone board smashed into pieces right in front of her.

Behind the pile of rubble appeared a short top-rank True God.

The man looked rather unpleasant. His eyes were shaped like bells, and his eyebrows resembled worms. He had what looked like sausages for his lips. He was very funny-looking.

"Well done! This woman is good! I wonder how she tastes like!" The top-rank True God laughed pretty hard.

The woman was in no mood to laugh at the funny-looking guy.

With just her strength, how could she put up a fight against a top-rank True God?

But she had no choice. After all, there was no one she could rely on.

With a face painted with desperation, she formed a profound sword tactic using her fingers. All of a sudden, the four purple swords charged towards the top-rank True God.

The fatal move of the Gods Killing Sword Array sure was something else.

Even some mid-rank True Gods in the divine land would find it difficult to withstand the power of the woman's four swords.

To her surprise, the top-rank True God opened his mouth which was filled with long white teeth. In the blink of an eye, he swallowed all of the swords.

He used the Heaven Devouring Technique. By practicing such skill, he could swallow almost anything he wanted to.

The top-rank True God rubbed his belly and laughed mockingly. "The internal momentum coming from the Purple Aura Godly Way is delicious! Chick, why did you come to the Cyan Demon Region?"

The woman immediately sank into despair.

She couldn't help but resent herself for being too weak. She only went there to see her husband in the Swirl Forest. She thought the people who resided closest to the forest could help her, but she was wrong.

She never thought it to be such a weird place.

If she didn't leave soon, she could die.

There was bitterness written all over her face. With a hint of sadness in her voice, she said, "I intend to enter the Swirl Forest to find my husband."

The Swirl Forest was a forbidden area of the divine land. Only members of the powerful clans on the Floating Islands were qualified to explore it.

That woman just said that her husband was inside the Swirl Forest.

The rest burst into laughter.

All of them laughed except the top-rank True God. He opened his mouth and asked, "Your husband? Which part of the Swirl Forest is he staying at?"
