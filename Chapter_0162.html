Chapter 162 Just A Fist



"Zheng is smiling?! He can laugh in the face of a terrifying demon!?"

"It's no big deal! He just passed through the first and second circles."

"Do you want to take a try? Look at the those people. Who dared to laugh like Zheng is doing now? Not even Henry is snickering!"

Henry's face paled at their words.

To confront the demon, he would force a smile if necessary. Still, it was clear that in this situation, it would be less embarrassing to smile than to cry. Deep down, he decided he had to find an appropriate time to teach Zheng a lesson since Zheng kept showing him up. As he schemed, Henry's mood improved.

The gossiping reached Zheng too, who felt a bit self-conscious. To circumvent more misunderstandings, Zheng changed his expression when he entered the second circle, scowling seriously.

Once he was in the second circle, Zheng became more threatened, but, he could still withstand the evil spirit.

Zheng kept moving forward, and soon enough, he had progressed through the third, fourth and fifth circles.

After he advanced into the fifth circle, the demon's tremendous torso shook, as it perked its ugly head up, roaring at Zheng.

An ordinary beast's roar wouldn't intimidate Zheng.

But, this demon was somehow more menacing.

On hearing the roar, Zheng felt his heart racing and he writhed in fear.

"Thump! Thump!"

Zheng's heart raced faster as the evil spirit circled him, eyeing him as though he was the only one alive, on earth or in heaven.

Feeling fear was a natural reaction if a person was trapped by a monster.

Apart from his heart thumping harder and faster, Zheng reacted with an involuntary shiver.

Humans instinctively avoided calamity and sought more comfortable venues instead. Sense made them shy away when faced with a risk or threat.

Zheng was human too, so, naturally, he felt the instinct as well.

Likewise, he could be afraid and flinch. Like anyone, he was bothered by negativity, which impacted him.

"Although the evil spirit in the demon is weird, this threat means nothing at all to me!" Encountering the demon, Zheng's eyes gleamed, reflecting his unyielding courage.

Feeling courageous, Zheng stepped toward the demon resolutely.

"Growl! Rawr!"

Demons were similar to human beings, and intelligent enough to understand that they would be killed if caught. In spite of that, the demon still felt the need to torture weak humans spiritually.

Before this encounter with Zheng, the demon felt that its evil spirit effectively scared humans shitless.

But this guy now seemed unaffected by its evil spirit. Given that, the demon was furious and roared very loudly.

The fierce evil spirit rushed at Zheng, surging like tides of water and tightly enveloped him.

The evil energy gripping Zheng fettered him, like invisible chains, pulling and tearing into Zheng. His courage waned, hindering his progress as he headed to the circle ahead.

As Zheng stepped into the seventh circle, he had to stop and close his eyes because he felt the pressure and found it a little difficult to endure the evil spirit.

"Zheng has done very well reaching the seventh circle, but, why has he stopped?"

"His eyes are closed. What on earth could he be thinking about at such a time? If he makes it to the eighth circle, he will be tied with Henry."

"Ah, but, I believe it'll be impossible for Zheng to reach the next circle. See how he's standing there in a daze. What's wrong? Is he scared senseless?"

Outer disciples murmured amongst each other.

Noting that other outer disciples either passed through each circle at a fast pace or had the shit scared out of them marching into the first few circles, they thought Zheng was an extraordinary exception, making him stand out from other disciples. How was he able to stand there, eyes closed, pondering his life while within the seventh circle?

How was it possible for Zheng to contemplate anything at a critical time like this one?

Deep in thought, Zheng reminded himself again and again that the demon in front of him posed no threat to him, and that his surreal fears that rose from deep inside were imaginary.

As he reminded himself, Zheng heard another voice within his head, telling him not to move forward. The demon was conquerable in Zheng's opinion.

Once he took a step forward, the demon would tear itself apart and fall into an abyss where reincarnation would be impossible for it.

A battle was waging silently, deep inside Zheng, as his will and instincts struggled against each other.

When the demon realized Zheng's struggle, it roared louder.

Its entire body grappled frantically, as though it were trying to free itself, to propel itself at Zheng and swallow him whole.

The onlookers had already stood back at a distance, but, watching the demon's incessant roaring as it strove to unleash more evil spirit made them flinch and step back again.

Only Zheng stood still and silent.

"To confront a demon, one must keep his mind absolutely calm," reminded Aura. She was standing close to Zheng. Aura added, "Just remember, this demon is equal to a nature creature. Can a nature creature scare you that much?"

'A nature creature?' Zheng thought, Aura's question gave him a measure of understanding. Searching his heart, Zheng was undoubtedly not frightened of strongmen in tier eight or nine at nature level like Fren.

Realistically, the demon Zheng faced now shouldn't frighten him either.

Yet, the evil spirit had almost penetrated into Zheng's entire body, making his will struggle against his natural impulses.

With that assessment, Zheng decided to use his willpower to suppress his instincts.

Common belief held that it was more challenging to conquer oneself than to transcend another.

Suddenly, Zheng was struck by a brilliant idea to counter-attack the demon.

"The state of forgetting myself!"

Zheng pushed his soul into a state of forgetting himself.

To lose himself completely, Zheng isolated his consciousness from his material form.

Now, even if Zheng feared the demon, he was able to control the reaction of his body calmly without the evil spirit affecting him at all.

In the blink of an eye, Zheng's negative emotions were dispelled, and with them, his fear vanished.

Zheng's eyes shot open, where neither courage nor fear was reflected.

Instead, his eyes were bright and pure with no emotions whatsoever.

Mentally and emotionally prepared now, Zheng headed through the eighth circle.

On taking two to three more steps, Zheng was in the ninth circle.

"Look, Zheng has made it to the ninth circle. He has surpassed Henry's record!"

"Can it be possible that he doesn't fear the proximity of the beast?"

"I would be terrified if it were me out there..."

As disciples exchanged comments, they watched in awe as Zheng would approach the demon if he took another six or seven steps ahead.

The closer Zheng got, the more it infuriated the demon. Piercing eyes blazed red, as it gnashed its sharp teeth in rage and postured as though it would devour Zheng once he was near enough.

Unaffected by the demon's display, Zheng advanced unhesitatingly. He passed from the ninth circle to the tenth one. As he was nearer, Zheng calmly looked the demon up and down.

Despite the savagery of the demon, Zheng had left his ego and serenely gazed at the demon before him like it were nothing more than a small cat or dog.

"Well done, Zheng. You passed the trial by reaching the tenth circle, and therefore, you shouldn't endanger yourself by drawing nearer to the demon," mentioned Aura.

Aura reminded Zheng because, although the demon was restrained, it could attack Zheng if he were to get too close to it.

Despite Aura's gentle reminder, Zheng proceeded towards the demon as if he hadn't heard her.

Zheng's motion to continue getting closer shocked the demon. This human being was actively resigning himself to death.

'But, how is this human being able to continue? Shouldn't he be only half-step into the nature level?' the demon wondered. Long before, the demon had lost count of how many weak human beings like Zheng it had killed and eaten.

'So be it human, since you resign yourself to death, I will fulfill your desire and swallow you whole!' the demon mused as he watched Zheng slowly approaching.

"Zheng, my brother, come back! You've reached the innermost circle. You don't need to go any further!" yelled Nory anxiously. His browline was furrowed with apprehension and sweat rolled down his face, terrified for his friend.

"Did Zheng become so scared that lost his mind? Has he resigned himself to death?" questioned another disciple.

Turning a deaf ear to what people said, Zheng continued advancing, getting closer to the demon.

When he was a mere foot from it, the demon tilted its head to the side, as it opened its big mouth baring its razor-sharp teeth and clamping down on Zheng's head.

Screams of horror and shock filled the plaza at the horrific sight.

As the demon was about to bite him, Zheng swung a fist at the demon.

Instantaneously, a dragon scale came alive, lighting up inside Zheng's mind.

Seven dragon scales exerted considerable force, making Zheng far more potent than any disciples at the same refining level.

So, how impressive would the strength be with over a hundred dragon scales lighting up simultaneously?

"Slam!"

Before the razor-like teeth could sink into him, everyone heard the sound of Zheng's fist crashing into the demon's head as it resonated through the place.

When Zheng punched the demon, he hadn't yet tapped into any life vitality.

It was solely his physical strength.

Nevertheless, his fist was as destructive as a tsunami crashing ashore.

In a flash, the demon disappeared.

"Glub!"

The demon gurgled as it transformed into vaporous blood in the blink of an eye because Zheng's mighty force extended into its body.

Its flesh turned to ashes as it disintegrated, and bones shattered into tiny bits.

Standing there, with mouths agape, the other outer disciples were shocked at the horrific scene.

Since the demon had been sealed and controlled, many of them were capable of killing the demon as long as they kept some distance from it.

Where those disciples failed was in the difficulty of overcoming their inner demons.

While some performed exceptionally well by reaching the seventh circle and a few even managed to make it to the eighth circle, but, none of those disciples could overcome their deepest fears.

Being more courageous than ordinary individuals, they could temporarily suppress fears, making it possible to reach circles as close to the demon as they could.

That wasn't the only stratagem Zheng used though. He was unaffected by the evil spirit after he employed the forgetting himself tactic.

Zheng's method made it possible for him to confront the demon and annihilate it with just his fist.
