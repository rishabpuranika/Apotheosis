Chapter 2396 Truth Of Dark And Light Chaos

← Prev Index Next →


Still in the air, Luo Zheng sent Yu Shengfeng spiraling down with a roll of his body.

He had been preparing for this for a long time. Therefore the power of his strikes should never be underestimated.

Caught off guard, Yu Shengfeng wasn't even able to react to this surprise attack.

Bang!

Quietly, Yu Shengfeng just lay in a huge pit, eyes staring out in awe at what just happened.

Just then, Luo Zheng landed steadily at the edge of the pit. A faint smile appeared on his face, and a hint of excitement flashed from his eyes.

When he used the Eight Smoky Melodies a few moments before, Luo Zheng discovered that he could fuse the Truth of Divine Dao into this particular skill.

Although Luo Zheng had been considering the use of the Truth of Divine Dao recently, he wasn't in a hurry to totally grasp it as the side hall was occupied by Joy. Thus, his understanding of the Truth of Divine Dao was still very shallow, and it didn't even amount to Luo Nian's understanding of it.

However, the principle of the Eight Smoky Melodies was free to be used.

So while employing it, Luo Zheng managed to fuse the Truth of Divine Dao as if it were an instinct already.

Unlike Joy who had understood half of the Truth of Divine Dao, Luo Zheng had no idea that the Truth of Divine Dao had to follow the natural course.

Now that he was fully aware and capable of harnessing this great power, it excited him to have a new skill.

At the beginning, Yudie had been very angry with the sparring, but seeing Luo Zheng smash Gus into the ground left a smile on her face. 'Serves you right, ' she thought to herself.

"This is... The Truth of Divine Dao! Where in the world did you learn it?" Jing Wuhan's eyes were full of disbelief.

On the other hand, Yu Shengfeng was just about to ask the same thing but he was frozen in shock. He opened his mouth to speak but closed it soon after so he didn't look too stupid.

Although his original plan was to pull punches to let Luo Zheng have a counterattack, he was a consummate True God, anyway.

Originally he wanted to teach Luo Zheng a lesson, but it turned out that it was the other way around. It was too embarrassing for Yu Shengfeng.

He didn't care about Luo Zheng. To him, Luo Zheng was a young boy from the universe. Even if he had inherited the Immortal Mansion, he was a mere newcomer to the divine land. Would he even be so lucky to already have the opportunity to learn the Truth of Divine Dao? This was unfair!

The Truth of Divine Dao was considered such a rare thing in the divine land. Even the Holy Beings rarely had the chance to learn about it, not to mention the consummate True Gods.

In fact, both Yu Shengfeng and Jing Wuhan had already learned about the Truth of Divine Dao years ago.

Back then, Holy Jay had handed a golden Sanskrit word to Gu Bei.

Then, Gu Bei spent much time prying into its deepest secrets, and eventually obtained an abridged cultivation method from it. This came to be Truth of Dark and Light Chaos, something that Gu Bei cultivated himself.

This particular cultivation method was translated from the Sanskrit, but it was only a small portion found at the middle of the entire text.

But Gu Bei, with his remarkable skill and talent, had already arranged an array of Eight Diagrams to calculate it. With the help of Luo Xiao's calculation abilities, he managed to complete the entirety of the text soon enough.

The Truth of Divine Dao was so refined that a single mistake in wording would affect it altogether, causing it to lose its power.

This particular Truth of Divine Dao, revised intricately by Gu Bei, was certainly not the real Truth of Divine Dao. However, it was extremely powerful and had to be used with caution.

The Divine Dao that were cultivated by Jing Wuhan and Yu Shengfeng were all passed down from Gu Bei. They benefited a lot as they cultivated the Truth of Divine Dao revised by their talented, skillful master.

Though the two disciples were completely pleased with it already, Gu Bei still had a few doubts and worries. "This Truth of Divine Dao is just a small paper boat that has been patched by me," he warned. "You might sail a distance in the boundless ocean, but it will sink one day. It's impossible to cross the great ocean in this way."

Jing Wuhan's and Yu Shengfeng' cultivation bases were at the consummation of True God Realm. With this, the great ocean beyond the Divine Dao was something they had to consider after becoming Holy Beings, so they didn't think too much about it in the present. It was already very lucky for them to get the Truth of Divine Dao from their master.

With the help of their makeshift Truth of Divine Dao, they were basically invincible in the Arena of Legends. Barely anyone could match their strengths and power.

The two of them grew very proud of their Truth of Divine Dao.

On the other hand, Luo Zheng had only been to the divine land for a few years.

They were puzzled with his ability to know the new Truth of Divine Dao.

Luo Zheng didn't answer their question. He shrugged and said with an awkward smile, "Well, let's just head out to the palace first, shall we?"

Yu Shengfeng climbed up from the pit, still embarrassed and infuriated. He paid attention to Jing Wuhan's expression and casually asked, "Why don't we spar again?"

As soon as he finished speaking, Jing Wuhan glared at him in annoyance,

"That's enough!" He rolled his eyes, completely done with Yu Shengfeng' attitude.

After Jing Wuhan had snapped, Yu Shengfeng decided to just keep quiet and obediently follow behind him.

On their way into the Immortal Mansion, Jing Wuhan asked Luo Zheng why he stayed in the Immortal Mansion instead of going to the Everest Spirit Mountain.

Luo Zheng, not wanting to make it a big deal, kept it short and explained what had happened after he entered the Floating Island.

"What! You're the anonymous guy everyone's talking about?!"

Gus just listened, bewildered as Luo Zheng told them about his consecutive wins in the Arena of Legends. His voice was as loud as thunderclaps, echoing in the square of the Immortal Mansion.

Jing Wuhan, with an angry look in his eyes, said, "Don't mind this idiot. Just continue."

"After I received Harold's message, I decided to go and visit the Everest Spirit Mountain, but the Astronomical Transit Day came before that and I was invited by the Sun Blind race. In the Time Sea Forbidden Land, Rachelle and others..." Luo Zheng continued.

As he told them about the jade seal, Yu Shengfeng proclaimed loudly once again, "You snatched that jade seal from Rachelle! What a good job!"

It was obviously sarcastic, and Jing Wuhan couldn't stand Yu Shengfeng anymore.

He pointed to the direction of the weapon refining workshop where Livingston was doing a hard day's work.

"What's that? An iron man? A puppet? Shit! What a weak puppet..."

Hiss!

As Yu Shengfeng remained distracted, an invisible fish line shot from Jing Wuhan's fishing rod and tied up Yu Shengfeng' legs. As he brandished the fishing rod lightly, Yu Shengfeng circled into the air and was dropped into the pond nearby.

Jing Wuhan let out a small chuckle and turned back to Luo Zheng. "Go on with your story please."

Looking over his shoulder, Luo Zheng felt a little sorry for Yu Shengfeng, but continued on with his story nonetheless.

He explained to them how he had managed to escape.

When he mentioned Estelle's use of the Scepter of Gods' Silence, Jing Wuhan stopped in his tracks. "So... it was because of you."

Since the entire divine land had been silenced by the power of the scepter, no one had escaped the influence including Jing Wuhan and Yu Shengfeng.

After that, they realized the fact that Estelle had been released from the dungeon.

"But, if I may ask, where did you learn the Truth of Divine Dao?"

Jing Wuhan'd been waiting to ask that question since he saw Luo Zheng use it the first time.

He knew how difficult it was to get the Truth of Divine Dao, so he was really curious as to how Luo Zheng was able to get it.

Even though many of the golden Sanskrit words had already been discovered, only a few of them were deciphered correctly, while only a handful were considered valuable.

"I think I should be thanking my son for this," Luo Zheng said with a proud smile.

Hearing this, Jing Wuhan's eyes grew wider and he was struck with confusion.

Although he didn't say anything about it, he already knew that Luo Zheng had moved his family here when he saw the several beautiful women residing in the Immortal Mansion. Luo Zheng had the right to do this, so no one really bothered him for doing so.

But did the Truth of Divine Dao have anything to do with Luo Zheng's son?

A few minutes later, Luo Zheng led Jing Wuhan into the side hall. He pointed at the golden Sanskrit words hanging on the wall and said, "The Truth of Divine Dao was deciphered by my son from the golden Sanskrit words left by our master," he explained. "I actually didn't know this until I returned to the Immortal Mansion."

Hearing this, Jing Wuhan's jaw dropped and he let out a little gasp. His gaze fell on Luo Nian who was not too far away from them. "He is only 16 years old," Jing Wuhan processed. "How was he able to decipher the golden Sanskrit words?"

Joy smiled softly. She had felt the same way earlier. She said, "Why not ask him to write a translation for you?"

She neatly tucked away what Luo Nian translated for her, not wanting to share it with the rest.

On the sidelines, Luo Nian grew quite curious about Jing Wuhan all along the way as he watched him riding his ox and throwing Yu Shengfeng into the pond.

His attention immediately perked up as he heard them speaking about him.

"Kid, do you mind writing another translation of the golden Sanskrit words for me?" Jing Wuhan asked politely.

A faint smile appeared on Luo Nian's face. He looked quite shy and hesitant, but he responded, "Of course I can. It's not that hard, but..." He stopped for a while and eventually smiled, "Is there any benefits for me to do that?"
