Chapter 1708 In A Stalemate



The power that Tianming displayed had impressed many Supreme Lords in the universe.

All of them wanted to draw him over to their respective sides, but Tianming was a member of the Thoughtless Minds? the fact had the four great clans thrilled.

Out of nowhere, Tianming suddenly made his way into the top five, something nobody was expecting.

The moment Tianming's Soaring Snake moved, the Supreme Lords were left to ponder.

Another sword beyond this universe had made an appearance.

This meant that such a young warrior was likely to change the very fabric of the universe in the future.

Nathan's eyes were brimming with worry as he watched the battle unfold.

Since the opening of the Illusion Battlefield, this was the first time he had made such an expression.

"Tianming is indeed very strong." Lawson couldn't help but laugh grimly.

"You need to teach him a lesson!"

With his calm demeanor, Nathan replied, "He has to defeat Luo Luo Zheng before facing me."

"Humph! I doubt Luo Luo Zheng would defeat Tianming anyway." The mere mention of Luo Zheng's name put Lawson in a bitter mood.

With the strength Luo Luo Zheng displayed against Shania, Lawson saw his strength in a whole new light.

Of course, it wasn't just Lawson. The other tens of thousands of warriors outside the picture had the same mind on the matter.

But Luo Luo Zheng smashed Lawson into ashes in a single punch. Lawson couldn't help but feel sour every time he thought of this.

After the battle ended, Kurt's figure appeared on the stone once again, with crossed knees and a calm look in his eyes.

Tianming's terrifying display of strength indeed made his state of mind waver.

At this moment, he was silently chanting a Buddhist scripture, holding back the power of the inner demon. He still needed to face his next opponent. Only by maintaining a calm state of mind would he be able to go any further.

Not long after, the mysterious woman's voice resounded once again.

According to the rules of the round-robin battle set by this woman, there were three battles left.

First, it was Luo Luo Zheng against Tianming, and then Nathan against Kurt. The victors of these two battles would then have to face each other.

"Luo Luo Zheng versus Tianming!"

After the cold voice resounded, a warm smile surfaced on Tianming's face.

Now, he knew what his mission was.

The arrow of his destiny pierced through the universe, right into the divine land.

In the universe, he had only one goal? to fight against Luo Luo Zheng.

Whoosh!

With a flash of light on the stone, Tianming vanished from where he stood and appeared on the Heaven Earth Board.

Soon after, Luo Luo Zheng also stepped onto the board.

And as Luo Luo Zheng came into view, Tianming was filled with emotion.

Luo Luo Zheng had grown so fast in the past that Tianming could only look up to him.

Now, he finally stood opposite Luo Luo Zheng.

And this time, Tianming had a seventy percent chance of winning this battle.

All things considered, this was already an extremely high chance of winning.

But Tianming didn't dare be the slightest bit careless. Luo Zheng's potential was terrifying in itself? Tianming was well aware that if he were to give his opponent a sliver of a chance, Luo Luo Zheng would most likely turn the tides in his favor.

For a moment, both of them were silent. Then, Luo Luo Zheng smiled. "Draw your sword."

To this, Tianming silently nodded his head. Then, the Soaring Snake was slowly unsheathed from its sheath. A brilliant silver light shone as a silver moon appeared in his hand.

Meanwhile, Luo Luo Zheng had also pulled out his Thunder Wind Divine Sword.

The weapons between the two were not equal by any means.

The Thunder Wind Divine Sword was a first-rank divine weapon. Even while it was considered one of the best first-rank divine weapons and was even listed on the Spirit Tablet, Tianming's sword was a treasure that went beyond the Spirit Tablet.

There was an undeniable large gap between the two.

But a sword did not make a swordsman.

Believing this, Luo Luo Zheng completely overlooked the fact.

And so, the two swordsmen drew their weapons.

Countless warriors in the universe had their eyes on them, holding their breath in anticipation.

It was clear that an intense battle was about to unfold. No one wanted to miss a single moment.

"Tianming should win this!"

"Luo Zheng's sword strike defeated Shania, so it's indeed extraordinary. But Tianming can dodge it with his speed."

"Yeah, I think so too!"

Tianming himself had judged that he had a seventy percent chance of claiming victory against Luo Luo Zheng.

And almost ninety-nine percent of the onlookers believed that Tianming would win.

During his fight against Kurt, Tianming had managed to give a shocking display of strength.

At this point, people had already neglected Nathan, who was supposedly the top Godly Genius? they were already inwardly assigning that title to Tianming.

Nathan had the Xuanyuan Clan, the strongest clan in the entire universe.

It was even more powerful than the Hidden Spirit Temple.

But one's family background wasn't the key point in determining their strength. Nathan might not be able to handle Tianming.

Slightly wielding his Soaring Snake, Tianming made a ripple of light appear beneath his feet. This ripple of light circled him for a moment before following the board to where Luo Luo Zheng stood.

The moment it spread out, Tianming took a step forward.

It seemed as though he was traveling through light.

He moved with incredible speed.

In the blink of an eye, Tianming was charging towards Luo Luo Zheng.

And this time, Luo Luo Zheng didn't retreat.

With a slight shake of the Thunder Wind Divine Sword, Luo Luo Zheng sent forth a series of Nether God Shadows, which charged straight at Tianming.

Whoosh! Whoosh! Whoosh!

The Nether God Shadows were basically Luo Zheng's avatars as they used all sorts of fist moves against Tianming.

"Break!"

In the past, these Nether God Shadows could create some trouble for the enemies.

But in this situation, Tianming merely waved his hand and a faint ripple appeared in the space before him.

The Nether God Shadows advancing towards him were instantly shattered and dissipated.

Crack! Crack! Crack!

Following the Nether God Shadows, countless rays of lightning like dragons appeared, ready to devour Tianming.

In the face of these lightning bolts, Tianming didn't even bother to dodge them. Instead, with the momentum of a soaring dragon, he charged straight ahead.

The Soaring Snake had already shot toward Luo Luo Zheng.

Cling!

The two swords clashed.

"You should probably use more power," Tianming stated calmly.

Under this strike, a crack appeared on Luo Zheng's Thunder Wind Divine Sword.

In front of the Soaring Snake, a first-rank divine weapon was merely a toy.

"I will!"

Luo Zheng's gaze turned extremely sharp.

Piercing sword intent was shot out in the blink of an eye.

"Gods-intimidating Strike!"

Luo Luo Zheng struck out with the sword without hesitation.

At this moment, the Thunder Wind Divine Sword, wrapped in sword intent, had the strength to compete head-on with the Soaring Snake.

But Tianming had only swung the sword abruptly, immediately blocking Luo Zheng's strike.

Ever since Luo Luo Zheng comprehended the Gods-intimidating Strike, no one had been able to block him.

It was too sharp, too cold-blooded.

But Tianming did it like it was nothing.

Clang!

Following a crisp sound, Tianming took three steps back, a faint smile surfacing on his face.

"The Emotion Closing is a very powerful move, but this sword strike is far from enough," he said with a shake of his head.

By relying on the Gods-intimidating Strike alone, Luo Luo Zheng perhaps wouldn't have even been able to deal with Kurt, let alone Tianming.

"Then I'll just have to do it again!"

Turning slightly, the Thunder Wind Divine Sword slashed down once more.

But Tianming merely raised his hand and deflected the strike with ease.

There were two swords against each other.

Without attacking, Tianming had strong defense with his speed.

No matter how exquisite or sharp Luo Zheng's sword strike was, Tianming's sword swiftly blocked it in the blink of an eye.

Clank, clank, clank, clank!

Both of their feet were nailed firmly to the board.

The two swords intersected with clear, melodious sounds.

At this moment, neither of them used much strength.

It was purely a game of technology.

But as it was, Luo Luo Zheng was always the attacker as Tianming was always on defense.

In the beginning, every strike of Luo Zheng's sword had been completely countered by Tianming. Even after numerous strikes, Luo Luo Zheng wasn't posing any threat.

This outcome wasn't a surprise for many of the martial artists.

While Luo Zheng's sword skill was indeed formidable, even he had a limit. No matter how profound his moves were, Tianming managed to block them instantly.

But after Luo Luo Zheng had made a hundred attacks, the tables were somehow turned.

Suddenly, Luo Zheng's long sword trembled and started drifting erratically.

He was well aware that he couldn't deal with Tianming with his regular attacks.

Tianming was too fast, after all. He could see through Luo Zheng's sword strikes and block them with ease.

So Luo Luo Zheng had no choice but to change his strategy. It was time for him to abandon his usual sword strikes.
